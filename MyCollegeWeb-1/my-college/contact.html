Government Degree College for Womens,

Government Degree College for Womens, Hyderabad, is a prestigious autonomous public college established in 1921, affiliated with Osmania University. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across Science, Commerce, and Arts disciplines. The college holds a high accreditation with a NAAC "A++" grade and is recognized by UGC and AICTE. Located on Begumpet, Hyderabad, it has a reputation for quality education and offers courses such as B.Sc, B.Com, BA, M.Sc, MA, and M.Com with admissions primarily merit-based via the Telangana DOST portal for UG and TS CPGET for PG courses. The college also has good infrastructure facilities like hostels, canteen, library, labs, and sports complex.
